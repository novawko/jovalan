About

Quick FAQ

How do I void a character?
Send a PM or comment to our TH page OR DM/message/email a Masterlist Moderator or the owner @CancriPlasm. Link the character and their masterlist entry, if you can't find the masterlist entry, provide a masterlist number instead. Provide us the reason to why they're being voided. We will check and confirm its them and its you that owns them. Then they will be voided!

How do I participate in this species?
Our species community is strictly Discord-based as of right now. You can find the Discord link in the topbar of this entire site if you're interested! We have custom NPC bots and use Unbelievaboat for our store/inventory/currency system!
